About

I partner with organizations to solve complex problems using advanced analytics, experimental design, and behavioral science. With a PhD from UW-Madison and experience at Fortune 100 companies, I extract meaningful patterns from diverse data sources ranging from multimodal data from biometric sensors, custom surveys, and large-scale behavioral datasets.

My background combines rigorous research with business impact. I've led teams of >20 junior researchers, secured $200K+ in competitive research funding, and translated behavioral insights into strategic recommendations at Procter & Gamble.

I'm passionate about wearable health technology and building data systems that put people first—whether that's improving user experience through behavioral insights or ensuring responsible AI development that centers human wellbeing.
